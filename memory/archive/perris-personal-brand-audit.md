## Brand Positioning Analysis

### Current State: Fragmented Identity
Right now, someone discovering you sees:
- Instagram: dancer/content creator
- LinkedIn: unclear professional positioning
- Substack: writer (but unclear what about)
- Threads: new/sparse
- Website: slow/spammy blog
- CyphrCam: no clear connection to your personal brand

**Problem:** There's no clear narrative thread connecting "Perris the dancer" to "Perris the founder" to "CyphrCam the product."

### Desired State: Unified Founder-Artist Identity
Every platform should tell the same core story:

> **"I'm a dancer who got frustrated with the tools available for practice. So I built my own. CyphrCam is the app I always wished existed - and now I'm sharing it with the dance community."**

This positioning gives you:
- **Credibility** - you're not an outsider, you're the community
- **Authority** - you understand the problems because you live them
- **Trust** - your personal journey IS the product story
- **Differentiation** - no one else in the dance tech space has this narrative

### Recommended Brand Positioning Statement
**For all platforms:**

> Perris Aquino (Akino) — Freestyle dancer and founder of CyphrCam, the recording app built for dancers who need to hear the music to feel the music. Documenting the journey of building tools for the dance community.

## Content Strategy for Superfans

### The "Building in Motion" Content Pillar System

**Pillar 1: The Builder's Journey (40%)**
- Behind-the-scenes of building CyphrCam
- Lessons learned as a non-technical solo founder
- Wins, failures, user feedback, iterations
- "Day in the life" of building an app while being an artist
- This is your Substack + LinkedIn primary content

**Pillar 2: Dance & Artistry (30%)**
- Your own practice sessions (recorded with CyphrCam - meta)
- Reflections on flow state, musicality, freestyle philosophy
- Community spotlights and collaborations
- CyphrCam user content (when available)
- This is your Instagram + Threads primary content

**Pillar 3: Personal Growth & Identity (20%)**
- Filipino-American identity exploration
- ADHD as creative advantage
- Human Design alignment journey
- Decolonization and systems thinking
- This is your Substack + deeper IG content

**Pillar 4: Community Building (10%)**
- Responding to community, sharing user stories
- Dance industry observations and commentary
- Collaborations with BTM, dance educators
- This weaves through all platforms

### Content Distribution Flow
```
CREATION:
   One deep idea per week (Substack long-form)
        ↓
DISTRIBUTION:
   → LinkedIn post (adapted for professional angle)
   → Threads post (conversational excerpt)
   → Instagram carousel or Reel (visual adaptation)
   → IG Stories (behind-the-scenes snippets)
```
