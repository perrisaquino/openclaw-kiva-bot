# CyphrCam Content & Automation Systems Roadmap

**🧠 Using Claude Opus 4.6** for strategic automation planning.

Based on Perris's brain dump, here's a comprehensive plan to automate his biggest pain points and turn scattered content into systematic growth engines.

## 🎯 CORE PROBLEM IDENTIFIED

Perris generates TONS of valuable content but it's scattered across:
- Instagram Stories (ephemeral, no data capture)
- Loom videos (buried, not repurposed) 
- Community posts (limited reach)
- Obsidian notes (private, not shared)
- Voice memos/yaps (unstructured)
- Readwise highlights (not synthesized)

**The solution:** Build systems that capture, synthesize, and distribute this content automatically.

## 🔄 AUTOMATION SYSTEM 1: CONTENT CAPTURE & REPURPOSING

### The Instagram Stories → Newsletter Pipeline

**Problem:** Perris yaps valuable insights in IG Stories that disappear in 24hrs
**Solution:** Systematic capture and repurposing workflow

#### Phase 1: Manual Capture (Immediate)
1. **Daily Story Archive** (Perris does this)
   - Save important Stories to Highlights categorized by theme
   - Screenshot/screen-record key insights immediately
   - Voice-to-text transcription for quote extraction

2. **Weekly Story Review** (Kiva can help)
   - Every Sunday, review week's Stories for newsletter content
   - Extract 2-3 key insights per week
   - Turn into email topics, blog ideas, Twitter threads

#### Phase 2: Semi-Automated (Month 2-3)
1. **Story Monitoring Tool** 
   - Tool like StorySaver or manual screenshots
   - Kiva reviews weekly and flags content-worthy moments
   - Extract quotes, insights, mini-tutorials

2. **Content Database** (Notion/Airtable)
   - All Story insights categorized: Movement Practice, CyphrCam Features, Philosophy, Behind-the-Scenes
   - Status: Raw → Processed → Published
   - Cross-reference with engagement data

#### Phase 3: AI-Powered (Month 6+)
- Voice-to-text automation for Stories
- AI extraction of key themes/insights
- Auto-generation of newsletter drafts

### The Loom Video Content Mine

**Problem:** Valuable Loom videos buried and never repurposed
**Solution:** Systematic extraction and distribution

1. **Loom Audit** (Week 1)
   - Catalog all existing Loom videos by topic
   - Identify top 10 most valuable for immediate repurposing
   - Extract transcripts using Loom's built-in feature

2. **Weekly Loom Processing** (Ongoing)
   - Every new Loom gets transcript extracted
   - Key insights pulled for newsletter/blog content
   - Best clips edited into Instagram Reels
   - Audio-only versions for potential podcast content

3. **Loom → Blog Post Pipeline**
   - Use transcripts as blog post outlines
   - Clean up, add structure, publish to Substack
   - Cross-post to personal website once fixed

## 🌐 AUTOMATION SYSTEM 4: MULTI-PLATFORM DISTRIBUTION

### Content Repurposing Matrix

| Source Content | Repurpose Into | Platforms | Frequency |
|----------------|----------------|-----------|-----------|
| Instagram Stories | Newsletter content | Email | Weekly |
| Loom videos | Blog posts | Substack, Website | 2x/month |
| Obsidian insights | Twitter threads | Twitter, Threads | Daily |
| Voice memos | Podcast clips | Instagram, YouTube | As created |
| User testimonials | Case studies | All platforms | Monthly |
| Weekly Lab prompts | Educational content | All platforms | Weekly |

### Platform Strategy

#### Substack (Primary Writing)
- **Why:** Better than broken WordPress, built-in audience
- **Content:** Long-form insights, behind-the-build stories
- **Frequency:** 1-2x per week
- **Cross-post:** To personal website once fixed

#### Threads (Testing Ground)
- **Why:** Lower stakes than Twitter, growing dance community
- **Content:** Daily insights, quick tips, philosophical thoughts
- **Strategy:** Test content themes, expand what resonates

#### Website Overhaul
- **Problem:** WordPress lag kills motivation
- **Solutions:** 
  1. **Quick fix:** Optimize current WordPress (hosting, plugins, themes)
  2. **Medium-term:** Migrate to faster platform (Webflow, Ghost)
  3. **Long-term:** Custom site built with coding skills
