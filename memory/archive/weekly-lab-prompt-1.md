# 🧪 Weekly Lab #1: "The First 30 Seconds"

**Welcome to the CyphrCam Lab.** This isn't a course. It's an experiment space for movement artists who want to push their practice.

Here's this week's prompt:

## The Experiment
Pick a song you've never danced to before. Don't preview it. Don't plan anything.

Just hit record on CyphrCam and see what your body does in the **first 30 seconds**.

No warmup. No prep. Just the raw, honest first response.

## Why This Matters
We spend so much time perfecting our movement that we forget what it feels like to just... respond. To let the body decide without the mind interfering.

This prompt isn't about looking good. It's about seeing what truth shows up when you haven't planned anything.
