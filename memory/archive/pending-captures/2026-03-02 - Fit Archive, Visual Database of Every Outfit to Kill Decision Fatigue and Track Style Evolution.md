# 📝 Fit Archive, Visual Database of Every Outfit to Kill Decision Fatigue and Track Style Evolution

**Date:** March 2, 2026 2:33 PM PST
**TL;DR:** Create a visual grid/database of all outfits over time. Solves two problems: decision fatigue when choosing what to wear, and tracking personal style evolution. Notion gallery view or similar. Add to Inventions note in Obsidian.

## 🎯 Original Reflection
I thought of another idea, super random, but I think it'd be cool to make a Notion database or some kind of grid view of all of my fits that I've ever made so I could see the evolution of my fashion. Also because I don't like thinking about things to wear and if I'm ever confused on what to wear, I have an archive of my fashion over time. I'm not sure if that could be made at Notion or if I could. I have an Obsidian note called Inventions where I share just different ideas for things that I want to create.
