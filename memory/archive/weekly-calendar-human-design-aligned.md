# Weekly Calendar Structure - Human Design Aligned

**💻 Using GPT-5.3 Codex** for Human Design optimization.

Based on Perris's Generator design, night owl energy, and current CyphrCam building phase.

## 🧬 HUMAN DESIGN FOUNDATION

**Type:** Generator  
**Strategy:** To Respond (don't initiate — wait for things to respond to)  
**Authority:** Sacral (gut feeling = truth)  
**Profile:** 2/4 Hermit-Opportunist (alone time to master + success through network)  
**Environment:** Blending - Caves (enclosed, blended spaces work best)  
**Energy Pattern:** Night owl, works best at night  

## ⚡ ENERGY OPTIMIZATION SCHEDULE

### DEEP WORK BLOCKS (Cave Time)
**Monday-Thursday: 8 PM - 2 AM**
- Primary CyphrCam development  
- Coding, debugging, feature building
- No interruptions, phone in another room
- Maximum 4-6 hour focused blocks

**Friday: 6 PM - 12 AM**  
- Week review and planning
- Content creation (Instagram, emails)
- Community management (Skool responses)

### RESPONSE TIME (Open Energy)
**Monday-Friday: 4 PM - 6 PM**
- Check emails, DMs, community posts
- Respond to opportunities that feel exciting
- Quick decision making on urgent items
- Social media engagement (commenting, replying)

**Saturday-Sunday: Flexible**
- No scheduled work - pure response mode
- Practice sessions with CyphrCam
- Content creation when inspired
- Community connection if energy allows

### RECHARGE BLOCKS (Hermit Time)
**Daily: 2 AM - 4 PM**
- Sleep: 2 AM - 11 AM (9 hours)
- Personal time: 11 AM - 4 PM
- Movement, reading, Obsidian notes
- NO work obligations during this time

## 🕐 DAILY MICRO-RHYTHMS

### MORNING TRANSITION (11 AM - 4 PM)
**Purpose:** Gentle transition from sleep to work energy

- **11 AM - 12 PM:** Slow wake up, tea, light reading
- **12 PM - 2 PM:** Movement/practice (recharge before work)
- **2 PM - 4 PM:** Obsidian notes, planning, light admin

**Key:** No pressure, no scheduled calls, just preparing energy for evening work

### RESPONSE WINDOW (4 PM - 6 PM)  
**Purpose:** Engaging with outside world before deep work

- Check all inbound communication
- Respond to what feels exciting (Generator strategy)
- Make quick decisions on opportunities
- Social media engagement

**Key:** This is "responding to life" time - follow sacral guidance on what deserves energy

### DEEP WORK BLOCK (8 PM - 2 AM)
**Purpose:** Cave time for maximum productivity

- No interruptions allowed
- Phone in airplane mode or another room
- Complex, focused work only
- Single-tasking on most important project

**Key:** This is when Generator energy is strongest and most focused

## 🚫 ANTI-PATTERNS TO AVOID

### Energy Drains (Misalignment Signals)
- **Morning meetings or calls** (not aligned with night owl energy)
- **Multitasking during deep work** (breaks Generator focus)
- **Saying yes without sacral check** (leads to resentment/frustration)  
- **Too many social obligations** (drains hermit energy)
- **Initiating instead of responding** (working against Generator strategy)

### Productivity Traps
- **Forcing work during low energy** (2 AM - 8 PM typically low focus)
- **Checking social media during deep work** (breaks cave energy)
- **Over-committing to social plans** (exceeds 2-3 day social battery)
- **Working on "should do" vs "excited to do"** (misalignment)

## 📈 OPTIMIZATION INDICATORS

### Alignment Signals (Working WITH design)
- Waking up excited about what you're building
- Time flying during deep work blocks
- Natural end points at 2 AM
- Responding vs. forcing
- Energy increasing through the work week

### Misalignment Signals (Working AGAINST design)  
- Forcing yourself awake for morning obligations
- Procrastinating during designated work time
- Feeling drained after social interaction
- Saying yes then resenting commitments
- Working on projects that feel "shoulds" vs. "wants"

- **Response accuracy:** What opportunities felt exciting vs. draining?

- **Winter:** More hermit time, deeper focus blocks

- **High-stress periods:** Increase recharge blocks
- **Launch periods:** Temporarily adjust for marketing needs
