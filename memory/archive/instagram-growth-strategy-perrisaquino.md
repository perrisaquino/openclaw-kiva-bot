### Daily Content Framework (Mix and Match)

#### **Content Type 1: "Building in Public" (3x/week)**

**Format:** Stories + Feed posts showing CyphrCam development

**Examples:**
- Screen recording of coding sessions with your voiceover
- "Today I fixed the bug that was breaking [feature]. Here's what I learned..."

- User feedback that made you rethink something
- Moments of doubt/excitement/breakthrough

**Why this works:** People love behind-the-scenes. Shows you're not just marketing, you're actually building.

#### **Content Type 2: "Creator Using Creation" (3x/week)**

**Format:** You dancing with CyphrCam, showing the value

**Examples:**
- "Testing a new location for practice" (CyphrCam session in unexpected places)
- "This song just hit different today" (Emotional moment captured via CyphrCam)

- "Blackout mode session" (you disappearing into flow state)

**Why this works:** Social proof. Shows the tool in authentic use by its creator.

#### **Content Type 3: "Movement Philosophy" (2x/week)**

**Format:** Talking head videos, movement clips, carousel posts

**Examples:**
- "What I learned about freestyle from building a tool for freestylers"
- "The difference between practicing for an audience vs. practicing for yourself"
- "Why I needed CyphrCam before it existed"
- Your insights about movement, flow, artistry

**Why this works:** Positions you as a thought leader, not just a tool builder.

#### **Content Type 4: "Integration Insights" (1-2x/week)**

**Format:** Vulnerable posts about the artist/entrepreneur journey

**Examples:**

- Dennis Rodman quote application to your journey

**Why this works:** Relatability. Many followers are navigating similar identity splits.
