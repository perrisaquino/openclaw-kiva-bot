# CyphrCam Buyer Personas - Market Sophistication Framework

Based on Eugene Schwartz's market awareness levels + testimonial analysis

## Market Sophistication Levels Applied to CyphrCam

### Level 1: UNAWARE (Don't know they have a problem)
**Market Size**: 60% of potential users
**Characteristics**: Casual dancers, beginners, people who "just move" without thinking about documentation or improvement

### Level 2: PROBLEM AWARE (Know something's wrong, don't know why)
**Market Size**: 25% of potential users  
**Pain Points**: Frustrated with recording themselves, feel disconnected from their practice, want to improve but don't know how

### Level 3: SOLUTION AWARE (Know what they need, evaluating options)
**Market Size**: 12% of potential users
**Shopping**: Comparing dance apps, video editing software, practice tools

### Level 4: PRODUCT AWARE (Know about CyphrCam but not convinced)  
**Market Size**: 2.5% of potential users
**Behavior**: Following on social, joined waitlist, maybe tried beta

### Level 5: MOST AWARE (Ready to buy, just need final push)
**Market Size**: 0.5% of potential users
**Status**: Beta users giving testimonials, word-of-mouth referrals

## Marketing Strategy by Sophistication Level

### Level 1 (Unaware) → Create Problem Awareness
**Channels**: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
**Content**: "Why don't you record yourself dancing?" educational content
**CTA**: Follow for dance tips, join community

### Level 2 (Problem Aware) → Solution Education  
**Channels**: Instagram posts, YouTube videos, email sequences
**Content**: "The hidden barriers keeping you from consistent practice"
**CTA**: Download app, join waitlist

### Level 3 (Solution Aware) → Competitive Differentiation
**Channels**: Comparison content, feature demonstrations  
**Content**: "Why CyphrCam vs. other dance apps/video editors"
**CTA**: Free trial, beta access

### Level 4 (Product Aware) → Social Proof & Urgency
**Channels**: Testimonial content, case studies, user-generated content
**Content**: Real user transformations, community highlights
**CTA**: Download now, limited beta spots

### Level 5 (Most Aware) → Remove Final Friction
**Channels**: Direct message, email, retargeting
**Content**: Onboarding tutorials, success stories, support
**CTA**: Complete setup, share experience
