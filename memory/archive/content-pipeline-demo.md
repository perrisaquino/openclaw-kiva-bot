## 💼 **LINKEDIN VERSION** (Professional)

**Hook**: "The moment I realized my creative tools were holding me back..."

**Content**:
"Last October, I was in the Portland Japanese Garden—one of the most inspiring places I've ever been.

I felt the impulse to move. To dance. To create.

But I couldn't play music out loud. Not there. Not with people around.

So I put in headphones, danced, and recorded it.

When I watched it back, there was no music. Just silence. I had to add a song afterward that 'kind of' matched.

I told myself 'it's art.'

But it wasn't what happened.

This moment taught me something crucial about tools and creativity:

→ When your tools force compromise, you're not creating—you're settling
→ The best solutions come from personal frustration with the status quo  
→ Sometimes you have to build what doesn't exist

That frustration led me to build CyphrCam—because dancers deserved better tools.

What tools in your industry force you to compromise on your best work?"

**Hook Rating**: 🔥🔥
**Platform Notes**: Include question for engagement
