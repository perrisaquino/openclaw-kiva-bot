### **CONTENT MATRIX CLASSIFICATION** 
*Based on Justin Welsh's Framework:*

**Content Type:**
- [ ] **Actionable** - Ultra-specific guide teaching HOW to do something
- [ ] **Motivational** - Inspirational personal/industry stories about extraordinary people
- [ ] **Analytical** - Informational breakdown explaining WHY something works
- [ ] **Contrarian** - Goes against common advice/beliefs  
- [ ] **Observation** - Hidden/secret/silent but important trend
- [ ] **X vs. Y** - Compares two entities/styles/frameworks/companies
- [ ] **Present vs Future** - Status quo vs future prediction
- [ ] **Listicle** - Useful list of resources/tips/mistakes/lessons/steps

**Content Function:**
