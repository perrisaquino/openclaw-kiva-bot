## 🎬 PRIORITY 2: INSTAGRAM STORY CAPTURE SYSTEM (Day 1)

### The Manual Workflow (Until we automate)

#### Daily (Takes 2 minutes)
1. **Before posting Stories:** Ask yourself "Is this valuable beyond 24 hours?"
2. **If yes:** Save to camera roll AND add to relevant Highlight
3. **Tag Stories** with quick voice memo: "Newsletter idea" or "Thread idea"

#### Weekly (Sunday, 15 minutes)  
1. **Review week's saved Stories**
2. **Extract quotes, insights, mini-tutorials** 
3. **Add to content database** (I'll create a simple template)
4. **Flag top 3** for newsletter/blog content
