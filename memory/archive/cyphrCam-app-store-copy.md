## App Store Description

### Short Description (for search/preview)
Record yourself dancing to music through headphones. CyphrCam automatically syncs your music to the video. No editing. No speakers. No noise complaints. Just dance.

### Full Description

**Put in your headphones. Hit record. Dance anywhere.**

CyphrCam is the only recording app built specifically for dancers. Play music through your bluetooth headphones or speakers while you record, and CyphrCam automatically syncs the audio to your video. No post-editing. No noise complaints.

**Built by a dancer, for dancers.**

Whether you're sessioning at home, filming a dance video in public, teaching a class, dancing with friends or drilling combos in your apartment at 2AM, CyphrCam lets you practice without disturbing people around you. 

**Core Features:**

• Automatic Music Sync - Your headphone audio syncs perfectly to video with zero editing
• Unlimited Recording Time - No cutoffs during your best rounds
• Blackout Mode - Turn off the screen and focus on feeling, not looking
• Personal Music Library - Upload your own tracks and build playlists
• Live Music Controls - Skip, pause, adjust volume while recording
• Silent Disco Mode - Share audio with AirPods so you can session with friends silently
• Front and Back Camera - Switch perspectives mid-session
• Auto-Orientation - Portrait or landscape, CyphrCam adjusts automatically
• Save to Camera Roll - Your videos, your phone, no accounts needed

**No social media features. No algorithms. No distractions.**

CyphrCam protects your practice. That's it.

**For freestylers, poppers, lockers, breakers, wavers, animators, and every dancer who needs to hear the music to feel the music.**

Practice anywhere. Capture everything. Edit nothing.

## ASO (App Store Optimization) Strategy

### Primary Keywords to Target
- dance recording app
- freestyle dance
- music sync video
- dance practice app
- silent recording
- headphone recording

### Competitor Gap
No existing app specifically targets freestyle dancers with automatic headphone-to-video music sync. Most competitors are either:
- General video recording apps (no music sync)
- Music video apps (lip-sync focused, not dance)
- Dance tutorial apps (learning, not practice recording)

CyphrCam owns a unique category: **silent dance recording with automatic music sync.**
