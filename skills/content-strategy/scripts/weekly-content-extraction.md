# 🎯 Weekly Content Pipeline Extraction

**Schedule:** Every Sunday 8:00 PM  
**Purpose:** Extract content ideas from week's reflections → feed Notion content pipeline

---

## 🔄 Extraction Process

### 1. **Scan Weekly Reflections**
- All Quick Capture files from past 7 days
- Focus on **progressive summarization patterns**:
  - **Bold text** → interesting elements
  - **Bold + Highlight** → CONTENT GOLD (highest priority)
  - **Checked boxes** → what resonated with Perris
  - **Personal notes/additions** → authentic voice insights

### 2. **Extract Content Ideas Using Frameworks**
**From Content Strategy Skill:**
- **Personal Brand** (consciousness-raising): Political awakening, systems awareness, creative polymath journey
- **CyphrCam** (dance community): Solo builder story, tool creation, authentic dance community needs
- **Hook Framework**: SEED (Share → Explore → Engage → Discover)
- **Voice Modes**: Match current authentic state from reflections

### 3. **Content Categories to Extract**
Based on Perris's content pillars:

**Builder's Journey (40%)**
- Solo developer challenges and breakthroughs
- Business model decisions and frameworks  
- AI collaboration and productivity systems
- Political consciousness integration in business

**Dance Community (30%)**  
- CyphrCam development insights
- Dance industry critiques and solutions
- Movement practice and artistry evolution
- Community building and authentic expression

**Growth & Systems (20%)**
- ADHD entrepreneurship strategies
- Progressive summarization and knowledge management
- Systems of oppression awareness and integration
- Personal transformation narratives

**Community Building (10%)**
- Reflection-based discussions and questions
- Vulnerability and authentic sharing
- Connection and belonging themes

### 4. **Notion Pipeline Integration**
**Content Ideas pushed to Notion with:**
- **Headline** (copywriting skill applied)
- **Content Pillar** (Builder/Dance/Growth/Community)  
- **Platform** (Substack/Threads/Instagram/LinkedIn)
- **Hook Strategy** (from SEED framework)
- **Source Reflection** (link back to original Quick Capture)
- **Priority Level** (based on progressive summarization intensity)
- **Authenticity Notes** (voice mode and emotional state context)

## 🎯 Content Extraction Rules

### High Priority Content (Move to Top of Pipeline)
- **Bold + Highlight** elements from reflections
- Multiple checked boxes indicating strong resonance  
- Cross-cutting themes appearing in 2+ reflections
- High emotional intensity or breakthrough moments
- Specific storytelling details (like "1.5-star reviews," "bed bugs")

### Content Hook Generation
**Based on Reflection Patterns:**
- **Puzzle Hook**: "Here's something weird I noticed about..."
- **Personal Stakes**: "The mistake that cost me $1000 taught me..."  
- **Pattern Recognition**: "Why every creative entrepreneur hits this wall..."
- **Reframe**: "What if your scattered thinking is actually..."
- **Learning Journey**: "From cultural appropriation to conscious business..."

### Voice Matching
**Extract emotional/energetic state from reflection to match content voice:**
- Frustrated/angry → sacred rage voice mode
- Breakthrough moment → integration voice mode  
- Questioning/curious → exploration voice mode
- Strategic/clear → systems voice mode

## 🔧 Automation Workflow

### Sunday Content Extraction Cron:

1. **Scan** `Journal/Daily/Quick Captures/` for files from past 7 days
2. **Analyze** progressive summarization patterns and checked boxes
3. **Extract** content ideas using content strategy frameworks  
4. **Generate** headlines using copywriting skill
5. **Categorize** by content pillar and platform fit
6. **Push** to Notion content pipeline with proper formatting
7. **Create** weekly content calendar suggestions
8. **Report** extraction summary to Perris

### Integration Points:
- **Obsidian**: Source reflections and progressive summarization patterns

- **Notion API**: Push formatted content ideas to pipeline
- **Quick Capture Templates**: Maintain bi-directional links
