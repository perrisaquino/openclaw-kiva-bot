# LINKEDIN TEMPLATE - Professional/Business Focus

## **HOOK STRUCTURES** *(First 2 lines are critical)*

**Pattern 1 - Contrarian:**
"Everyone says [common belief].
But [contrarian insight] is actually true."

**Pattern 2 - Story:**
"I [specific action/experience].  
Here's what I learned about [professional topic]."

**Pattern 3 - Question:**
"Why do [specific group] struggle with [specific problem]?
After [experience], I think I know."

**Pattern 4 - List/Framework:**
"[Number] [actionable insights] about [topic]:
(Thread/breakdown below)"

## **BODY STRUCTURE** *(LinkedIn algorithm-optimized)*

### **Paragraph 1:** Hook (2 lines max)
### **Paragraph 2:** Context/Setup (2-3 lines)
### **Paragraph 3:** Core Insight/Story (3-4 lines)
### **Paragraph 4:** Application/Lesson (2-3 lines)  
### **Paragraph 5:** CTA/Question (1-2 lines)

## **LINKEDIN VOICE ELEMENTS**
- ✅ Professional but personal
- ✅ Business lessons from personal experience  
- ✅ "Here's what I learned..." / "This changed how I think about..."
- ✅ Specific numbers, results, examples
- ❌ Corporate jargon or buzzwords
- ❌ Overly casual/slang language
- ❌ Pure personal stories without business relevance

## **LINKEDIN BEST PRACTICES**

### **Format:**
- **Length:** 150-300 words (optimal engagement)
- **Paragraphs:** 1-2 sentences max
- **Line breaks:** White space for readability
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant ones at end
- **Emojis:** Sparingly, only when natural

### **Engagement:**
- **CTA:** Ask specific questions
- **Comment strategy:** Respond within first hour
- **Tag relevant people:** Only when genuinely relevant
- **Share timing:** 8-10 AM or 5-6 PM PST

### **Algorithm Optimization:**
- **Native content:** Text performs better than link posts
- **Comments > Likes:** Prioritize meaningful engagement  
- **First hour crucial:** Most engagement happens early
- **Consistency:** Post 2-3x per week minimum
