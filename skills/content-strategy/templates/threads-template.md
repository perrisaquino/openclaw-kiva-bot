# THREADS TEMPLATE - @iamperris

## **THREADS VOICE** *(More casual than LinkedIn, more thoughtful than Twitter)*
- ✅ Conversational and approachable
- ✅ Real-time thoughts and observations  
- ✅ Behind-the-scenes moments
- ✅ Questions that spark discussion
- ❌ Not corporate or overly polished
- ❌ Not purely promotional
- ❌ Not controversial for controversy's sake

## **POST TYPES & STRUCTURES**

### **1. THREAD FORMAT** *(Multi-post series)*
**Post 1 (Hook):**
"[Intriguing statement/question]

Thread: 🧵"

**Post 2-4 (Body):**
- One insight per post
- 2-3 sentences max
- Specific examples/details

**Final Post (CTA):**  
"What's your experience with this?

[Optional: Link to longer content]"

### **2. SINGLE POST FORMAT**
**Structure:**
- **Hook:** First sentence grabs attention
- **Context:** 1-2 sentences setup  
- **Insight:** Core point/observation
- **Question:** Engage audience

**Length:** 100-200 characters (optimal engagement)

### **3. VISUAL POST FORMAT**
- **Image + Caption:** Behind-the-scenes of building/dancing
- **Story context:** What's happening in the image
- **Lesson/insight:** What this teaches about [topic]
- **Question:** Connect with audience experience

## **THREADS BEST PRACTICES**

### **Timing:**
- **Peak hours:** 9-11 AM, 7-9 PM PST
- **Consistency:** 1-2 posts daily
- **Engagement window:** First 30 minutes crucial

### **Engagement Strategy:**
- **Reply quickly:** Within first hour of posting
- **Ask questions:** Every 3rd post should invite response
- **Support others:** Comment meaningfully on relevant posts
- **Hashtags:** 2-3 max, use sparingly

### **Algorithm Tips:**
- **Native content:** Text + native images perform best
- **Replies matter:** Quality comments boost reach
- **Saves important:** Content people want to reference later  
- **Avoid external links:** Unless essential (algorithm penalty)
