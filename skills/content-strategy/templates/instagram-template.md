# Instagram Content Template

## Post Structure
- **Hook** (first line grabs attention)
- **Value/Story** (2-3 lines max)
- **Call to Action** (clear next step)
- **Hashtags** (5-10 strategic)

## Caption Template

🎯 **HOOK LINE** (something that stops the scroll)

[2-3 lines of value - story, insight, or lesson that connects to your dance/building journey]

**What's your experience with [relevant question]?** 👇

---
#freestyle #dance #cyphrcam #buildinginpublic #solodev #movement #dancecommunity #entrepreneurship

## Voice Guidelines
- **Casual + intentional** (not corporate, not overly casual)
- **Questions engage community** 
- **Stories > abstract concepts**
- **"Dancer who built the tool dancers need"** positioning
- **Avoid "content creator" language** - you're a builder/artist

## Content Pillars (Instagram Distribution)
- **Builder's Journey**: 40% - Behind scenes of building CyphrCam
- **Dance Practice**: 30% - Movement, flow state, technique insights  
- **Growth Systems**: 20% - Lessons from entrepreneurship/artistry merge
- **Community**: 10% - Responding to/amplifying other creators

## Hashtag Strategy
**Core Tags (always use 3-4):**
- #freestyle #cyphrcam #buildinginpublic #solodev

**Rotation Tags (pick 3-5 per post):**
- Dance: #dancecommunity #urbanstyles #flowstate #movement #streetdance
- Tech: #iosdev #appdev #swiftui #indiehacker #techforants
- Business: #entrepreneurship #alignment #consciousness #thriving

## CTA Options
- **Engagement**: "What's your experience with [topic]? 👇"
- **Story**: "Ever felt this way? Tell me in comments"
- **Educational**: "Which tip resonates most?"
- **CyphrCam**: "Beta testing soon - comment 'FLOW' for early access"
