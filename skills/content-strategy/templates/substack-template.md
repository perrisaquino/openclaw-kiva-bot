# SUBSTACK TEMPLATE - "The Cyphr" 

## **HEADER STRUCTURE**
- **Subject Line:** [Clear, benefit-driven headline]
- **Subtitle:** [Supporting context in 1 line]
- **Read Time:** [X min read]

## **OPENING HOOK FRAMEWORKS**
*(Choose one based on content type):*

**Personal Story Hook:**
"I was [situation] when [unexpected thing happened]. What happened next changed how I think about [topic]."

**Contrarian Hook:** 
"Everyone tells you to [common advice]. I used to believe that too. Until [experience] taught me the opposite."

**Question Hook:**
"What if everything you know about [topic] is backwards? Let me share what I learned from [experience]."

## **BODY STRUCTURE** *(Substack-optimized)*

### **Section 1: Setup**
- Context/background (2-3 sentences)
- Why this matters to reader
- Personal stake/credibility

### **Section 2: The Story/Insight** 
- Core narrative or key insight
- Specific details that make it memorable
- What changed/what you learned

### **Section 3: The Broader Truth**
- Universal principle/lesson
- How it applies to reader's life
- Connections to bigger themes

### **Section 4: Application**
- Practical takeaways
- Action steps (if applicable)
- Questions for reflection

## **CLOSING STRUCTURE**
- **Summary:** Key takeaway in one sentence
- **Question:** Engage readers (What's your experience with X?)
- **CTA:** Subscribe/share/comment
- **P.S.:** Personal note or behind-the-scenes

## **VOICE ELEMENTS** *(From content strategy skill)*
- ✅ Conversational, not corporate
- ✅ "I noticed..." / "Here's what I'm learning..."  
- ✅ Specific details over generalizations
- ✅ Questions that make readers think
- ❌ No "Great question!" or filler phrases
- ❌ No em dashes (—) ever
- ❌ No forbidden language from analysis

## **SUBSTACK BEST PRACTICES**
- **Length:** 800-1500 words (ideal engagement)
- **Paragraphs:** Short (1-3 sentences max)
- **Lists:** Use bullet points for readability
- **Subheadings:** Break up long sections
- **Links:** External links open in new tabs
- **Images:** 1-2 relevant visuals max

## **SUCCESS METRICS**
- **Open Rate:** 40%+ (good for personal brands)
- **Click Rate:** 5%+ on links
- **Comments:** Quality engagement over quantity
- **Shares:** Organic sharing (avoid asking directly)

*This template ensures consistent voice and structure while maintaining authenticity and engagement.*
