# TikTok Content Template

## Video Structure (15-60 seconds)
- **Hook** (first 3 seconds - text/visual that stops scroll)
- **Setup** (3-10 seconds - context/problem)
- **Payoff** (10-45 seconds - solution/insight/demo)
- **CTA** (last 5 seconds - follow/comment/check bio)

## Content Formats

### 1. Behind-the-Scenes Building
**Hook**: "Building an app as a dancer hits different..."
**Setup**: Show code/problem you're solving
**Payoff**: Demo the feature working
**CTA**: "Building in public - follow the journey"

### 2. Dance + Tech Combo
**Hook**: "I solved every dancer's camera problem..."
**Setup**: Show traditional filming struggles
**Payoff**: CyphrCam demo/before-after
**CTA**: "Beta coming soon - comment 'FLOW'"

### 3. Alignment Stories
**Hook**: "2 years ago I hated my business..."
**Setup**: Quick story of misalignment
**Payoff**: What changed / current state
**CTA**: "Alignment > hustle - what's your story?"

### 4. Movement Insights
**Hook**: "Flow state secrets dancers know..."
**Setup**: Common misconception/problem
**Payoff**: Technique/mindset shift
**CTA**: "What gets you in flow?"

## Trending Format Adaptations

- **"Tell me without telling me"**: Tell me you're in flow state without telling me (show movement)

- **"Plot twist"**: Thought I'd be a traditional entrepreneur... plot twist: built tools for artists

## Video Production Notes
- **Vertical 9:16** (phone orientation)
- **Good lighting** (natural preferred)
- **Clear audio** (if talking - use external mic or get close to phone)
- **Quick cuts** (keep attention - edit every 2-3 seconds)
- **Text legible** (large, contrasting colors)
- **Captions** (for accessibility + sound-off viewing)
