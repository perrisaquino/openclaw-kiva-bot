# YouTube Content Template

## Video Types & Formats

### 1. Long-Form (10-30 minutes)
**Deep dive content for subscribers who want full context**

### 2. YouTube Shorts (15-60 seconds)  
**TikTok-style for discovery (can repurpose TikTok content)**

### 3. Medium-Form (3-10 minutes)
**Focused tutorials, insights, behind-the-scenes**

---

## Long-Form Video Structure

### Opening (0-30 seconds)
- **Hook**: "In this video, I'm showing you [specific outcome]"
- **Promise**: "By the end, you'll understand [value]"  
- **Preview**: "First we'll cover [A], then [B], and finally [C]"

### Body (80% of video)
- **Segment 1**: Setup/Context (why this matters)
- **Segment 2**: Deep dive/demonstration
- **Segment 3**: Insights/lessons learned
- **Segment 4**: How to apply this

### Closing (last 2 minutes)
- **Summary**: "So to recap..."
- **CTA**: "If this helped you, subscribe for more"
- **Next video tease**: "Next week I'm covering..."

## Thumbnail Strategy

### Builder Content
- Split screen: You + code/app interface
- Progress shots: Before/after app development
- Text overlay: "Week [X]" or "FINALLY WORKS"
- Color scheme: Tech blues/greens with your face

### Dance Content  
- Movement shots: Mid-dance captures
- Split: Traditional filming vs CyphrCam result
- Text: "EVERY DANCER NEEDS THIS" type hooks
- Color: Vibrant movement energy colors

### Alignment/Business
- Professional but authentic photo of you
- Text overlay with key insight
- Clean, minimalist design
- Colors that reflect consciousness/growth

## SEO Strategy

### Keyword Focus
**Primary**: dance app, freestyle dance, building in public, solo developer
**Secondary**: flow state, movement practice, conscious entrepreneur, dancer tools
**Long-tail**: "app for freestyle dancers", "building app for dancers", "dance practice tools"

### Optimization
- **Title**: Include main keyword in first 60 characters
- **Description**: Include keywords naturally in first 2 lines
- **Tags**: Mix broad and specific (dance, dance app, CyphrCam, freestyle, etc.)
- **Custom thumbnail** with clear, readable text
