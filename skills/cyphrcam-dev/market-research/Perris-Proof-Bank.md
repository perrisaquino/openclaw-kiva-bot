# Perris Proof Bank — Authority & Credibility Elements

*For strategic use in copywriting. NEVER boast. Only deploy when a proof point naturally strengthens the argument being made.*

## RULE: How to Use This Bank

**DO:** Weave proof points in when they preface or support a specific claim. The credibility should serve the story, not interrupt it.

**DON'T:** List credentials for the sake of listing them. If removing the proof point doesn't weaken the argument, it doesn't belong there.

**Example of GOOD use:** "After 20 years of dancing, I'm still discovering things about my own movement through Cyphr footage" — the 20 years gives weight to the discovery.

**Example of BAD use:** "I'm a 20-year dancer who worked with the Jabbawockeez and here's my app" — that's a resume, not a story.
