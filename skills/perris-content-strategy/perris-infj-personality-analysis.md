- **Take on too many problems** - endless supply available
- **Self-neglect as warning sign** of self-esteem needing attention
- **Burnout threatens** when working in overdrive to help others

**When Confidence Shaken:**
- **May push past agendas too hard** or become artificially brash
- **Abandon characteristic open-mindedness** and ignore others' wishes
- **May become self-absorbed** and intolerant of ideas not their own
- **Identify so closely with ideals** that criticism feels like attack on principles

### Self-Care & Boundaries
- **Schedule time and energy** to include robust personal care
- **Calendar to track personal needs** - exercise, rest, recreation, relationships
- **Treat self-care appointments** like work or family obligations
- **Learn to say 'no'** to optional requests to preserve vitality

### Growth Integration
- **Focus on failure as lesson** rather than inadequacy indicator
- **Reframe expectations** - "This hasn't succeeded... yet"
- **Celebrate small steps forward** and allow restorative periods
- **Balance future focus** with appreciation of present moment

### Confidence Restoration
- **Connect purpose and principles to actions** consciously
- **Take inventory of how actions match values** regularly
- **Handle criticism objectively** - separate specific feedback from identity attack
- **Remember criticism may not be personal** or universal about identity
